Hello, friend.




I'm a Stanford student interested in natural language processing (NLP) and computational social science. My research aims to better understand people through language.

Right now I'm doing an independent study with the Stanford NLP group, advising students for SymSys, and course assisting CS 224U: Natural Language Understanding.

Last summer, I was a Summer@EPFL student in the Data Science Lab with professor Robert West. I've also worked with the Stanford SNAP Group, mentored by University of Michigan professor David Jurgens.

I'm passionate about education and increasing diversity in science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM). Check out this blog series I made on teaching NLP.

Pronouns: she/her
